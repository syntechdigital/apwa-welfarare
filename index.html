About APWA

Ameru Pwani Welfare Association, (APWA) is a Pwani-based welfare group dedicated to supporting affordable living for residents and communities. We stand together in funerals, weddings, and celebrations, ensuring dignity and unity.

Our History

Founded in Unity

Started as a small welfare initiative, APWA grew into a strong community pillar.
